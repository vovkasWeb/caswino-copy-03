Common Questions

Prizes up to AU$5,000 are instantly credited to your RooSpinny account. For larger amounts, you'll need to fill out a claim form and verify your identity. Jackpot winners are contacted by our team within 24 hours of the draw.

All prizes must be claimed within one year from the draw date.

The odds of winning any prize in RooSpinny are 1 in 15. The jackpot odds are about 1 in 8 million. Lower prize tiers have better odds:

  • Match 3: 1 in 56
  • Match 4: 1 in 1,032
  • Match 5: 1 in 55,492

Currently, RooSpinny is only open to players who are physically in Australia. You must be at least 18 years old and provide valid Australian ID for prizes over AU$1,000.

We hope to expand internationally soon—stay tuned!
