Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

At RooSpinny.com, your privacy is extremely important to us. This document details how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit or interact with our website.

Information We Gather

We may collect personal details you provide directly—such as your name and email—when you sign up, participate in offers, or contact us. We also collect non-personal data like device type, browser, and IP address to improve your experience and our services.

How We Use Your Data

Your information may be used for the following reasons:

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To respond to your questions or support requests
  • To send you updates, promotions, and important notifications
  • To comply with legal obligations

Cookies

We use cookies to personalize your experience and analyze site usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect some features.

Third-Party Partners

We may work with third-party providers for analytics, marketing, or payment processing. These partners have their own privacy policies, which we recommend you review.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to safeguard your personal data. However, please be aware that no method of online transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@RooSpinny.com.

Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please email us at support@RooSpinny.com.
